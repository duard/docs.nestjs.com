Purpose

Here to help people to manage their nice projects, the @nestjs/cli tool is here to help you.
From scaffolding the project structure to build well define architecture and your project.

NestjsCLI is based on the @angular-devkit. A special schematics are dedicated for the Nestjs development environment @nestjs/schematics
